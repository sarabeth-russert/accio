Hogwart's Adventures: Accio



Are you ready to go on your very own Wizarding World adventure?

Hogwart’s Adventures: Accio, is your very own interactive wizarding game application. It’s a virtual book that takes you on a search for unique magical items.

You start at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and explore popular locations like:

· Diagon Alley
· The Forbidden Forest
· Hogsmeade
· Professor Dumbledore's Office

Meet with different wizards, witches and creatures, collect hints to solve questions and acquire ingredients for a magical potion.

Your enchanted adventure awaits, log in now!
